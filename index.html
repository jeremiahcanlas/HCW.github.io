We help you embrace your independence

Homecare on Wheels is a health care organization providing various care services such as: Homemaking, Personal Support Care (PSW), Nursing, Geriatric Clinical Nurse Specialist, Caregiver Respite Relief, In-Home Physician/Nurse Practitioner virtual/in person visits. Our Health Care organization provides Leadership and management consultation to health care organizations to avoid practice risks and compliance orders.

Years of service in the healthcare industry has led us into variety of settings from hospitals, long term care facilities, retirement homes and community care.  Our staff comprises of Homemakers, Personal Support Worker, Registered Personal Nurse, Registered Nurse, Clinical Nurse consultant and much more who can provide relief for your staffing shortage.  Therefore, if you are struggling to fill your staffing quota, you have come to the right agency because we can help.

About Us

Home Care on Wheels Inc. was founded by a group of four persons, among whom are delegated two Registered Nurses with experience in a multitude of settings, including hospitals, long term care facilities and community health initiatives.

The knowledgeable staff at Home Care on Wheels have provided care services and treatments in chronic care diseases, injuries, Motor Vehicle Injury victims, rehabilitation services and age-related illnesses.  We take great pride and commitment in delivering health care expertise with a personal touch.  Every client is “an individual” not a number and deserves to be treated with respect and dignity.

Home Care on Wheels management team will work with each client on an individual basis and are available 24/7 to answer any questions regarding the services we can provide.  We are committed to helping you navigate the various services, make referrals within the health care system regarding your health care issues.
